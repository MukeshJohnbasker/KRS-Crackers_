Safeguarding Smiles  : Celebrate Diwali Responsibly

  •  Store fireworks in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent unintentional ignition.
  •  Dispose of used fireworks carefully by soaking them in water before placing them in a designated fireproof container.
  •  Keep a bucket of water or a hose nearby when setting off fireworks to quickly extinguish any unexpected fires.
  •  Choose open, outdoor spaces for lighting fireworks, away from buildings, trees, and other flammable materials.
  •  Educate neighbors about your Diwali celebration plans, especially if they have pets or individuals sensitive to loud noises, promoting a considerate and inclusive celebration.
